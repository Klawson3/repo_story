# Chapter 2
Pip stroked his chin, pretending to ponder. "Hmm, that's a 'piercing' question. The answer is a needle!"

The goblin grumbled as the path cleared. "Next! What is always in front of you but can’t be seen?"

"Ah, a classic! That would be the future," Pip replied with a wink.

The second goblin stamped his foot in frustration as another part of the path disappeared. "Last one, little wizard! What has to be broken before you can use it?"

Pip grinned. "This is 'eggs-actly' what I needed! The answer is an egg!"

With the riddles solved, the path cleared, and Pip continued his journey. Soon, he reached the River of Lost Thoughts, its waters murky and swirling with forgotten memories. A grumpy ferryman, a troll named Gruff, guarded the only boat.
